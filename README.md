SADA is an agentic AI platform designed to help businesses proactively identify user friction before launching products or services. Instead of relying on post-launch analytics, customer complaints, or reactive UX fixes, SADA simulates thousands of human experiences to understand how real users feel, behave, and fail when interacting with a product.

Modern products often fail not because they are technically broken, but because they introduce small, invisible frictions like accessibility gaps, cognitive overload, unclear flows, cultural mismatches, or emotional stress. These issues only surface after launch, when businesses lose users, revenue, and trust. SADA shifts this process left by allowing teams to test human experience at scale before real users are impacted.
